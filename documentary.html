Chasing Greatness: Henry County Basketball

Chasing Greatness, Henry County Basketball is a documentary series that follows the Henry County High School boys basketball team during the 2024-25 season and highlights the basketball journey of head coach Derek Tingle.

I filmed, produced and edited the entire docuseries over the course of 14 months. Each episode is 30-35 minutes in length, with a total run time of about 235 minutes.
